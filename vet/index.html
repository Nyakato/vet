Our Specialties

Bath and Brush

Begins with a relaxing, natural shampoo to rejuvenate skin & coat. Next we trim nails & clean ears. Your pet is then fluffed dry, brushed & combed. Finished with choice of bandana or bow and cologne..

Full body groom

Includes all features of the Bath & Brush, plus full clipper and/or scissored hair style customized to fit your pets' lifestyle. Pricing varies based on the breed & level of work involved..

Blueberry wellness

Blueberry Facial, Soothing Massage, Specialty Shampoo, Re-Hydrating Conditioner, Deodorizing Ear Flush, Nail File, Anal Gland Expression, Teeth Cleaning & Breath Freshening.

Mini groom

Includes all features of the Bath & Brush, then additional trimming of face, feet, fanny, ears and tail. This also helps prolong your pets' complete groom.
